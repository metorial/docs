Once you have your providers deployed and integrated, you'll want to keep an eye on their activity and health.

<Note>
  **What you'll learn:**

  - How to view activity, sessions, connections, and tool calls
  - How to inspect provider runs and errors
  - How to review alerts, Protoguard events, and provider-auth events

  **Before you start:**

  - Complete the [Introduction](/metorial-101-introduction) guide
  - Complete the [Using your first provider](/metorial-101-deploying) guide
  - Complete the [Testing your first provider](/metorial-101-testing) guide
  - Complete the [Using your first provider](/metorial-101-using) guide
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Open Logs Activity">
    Navigate to **Logs** in the sidebar to see recent activity across the project.

    ![Logs activity](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-logs-activity.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. Review Sessions and Connections">
    Use **Sessions** for provider sessions and **Connections** for connection-level records.

    ![Sessions log](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-logs-sessions.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="3. Inspect Tool Calls and Provider Runs">
    Use **Tool Calls** for individual tool invocations and **Provider Runs** for provider execution records.

    ![Tool calls log](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-logs-tool-calls.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="4. Review Errors and Alerts">
    Use **Errors** and **Alerts** to find failed provider runs and operational warnings.

    ![Provider errors](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-logs-provider-errors.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="5. Audit Auth Events">
    Provider auth events and errors have their own pages for OAuth, API key, and setup-session debugging.

    ![Provider auth events](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-provider-auth-events.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
