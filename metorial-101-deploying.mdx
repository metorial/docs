## Deploying a provider

Providers in Metorial provide tools and resources to AI assistants through the Model Context Protocol.

Provider deployments can be locked to a particular version of a provider, which guarantees that the capabilities stay the same. By default, a deployment always points to the latest version.

This guide will walk you through deploying your provider and making it accessible to AI applications.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Provider Page">
    Navigate to the **Providers** section in the dashboard and select the **Metorial Search** provider.

    ![Step1](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deployment">
    Click **Deploy Provider**.

    ![Step2](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Deployment Settings">
    Set up your deployment  by choosing a clear, descriptive name for it.

    ![Step3](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Provider is Ready">
    The provider has now been deployed and is ready to use!

    ![Step4](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
