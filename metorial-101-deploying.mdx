## Deploying a provider

Providers in Metorial provide tools and resources to AI assistants through the Model Context Protocol. This guide will walk you through deploying your provider and making it accessible to AI applications.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Provider Page">
    Navigate to the **Providers** section in the dashboard and select the **Metorial Search** provider.

    ![Provider Overview](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step-1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deployment">
    Select **Deploy Provider**.

    ![Provider Deployment Creation](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step-2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Deployment Settings">
    Set up your deployment configuration by choosing a clear, descriptive name for the deployment.

    ![Deployment Configuration](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step-3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Provider is Ready">
    The provider has now been deployed and is ready to use!

    ![Testing Deployed Server](/images/metorial-101/deploying-mcp-servers/step-4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
