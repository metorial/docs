## Start With Workforce

Metorial helps teams give AI apps, clients, and agents safe access to the apps and systems employees use every day. Workforce is the control plane: admins approve integrations and skills, employees connect through portals, AI clients use managed MCP URLs, and activity logs show access events, tool calls, and usage.
