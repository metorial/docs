Start by creating the workspace that will hold your portals, integrations, skills, accounts, agents, and activity logs.

<Note>
  **What you'll learn:**

  - How to sign in to Metorial
  - How to create an organization
  - How to choose a data region
  - How to create the first project for Workforce
</Note>

## Sign In

Open the [Metorial Dashboard](https://platform.metorial.com) and sign in with your account.

![Metorial login](/images/dashboard-audit/auth-login.png)

## Create An Organization

An organization is the top-level workspace for your team. It contains projects, members, billing, and shared settings.

Choose the data region that fits your users and compliance needs.

![Create an organization and choose a data region](/images/dashboard-audit/onboarding-create-organization-eu.png)

## Create A Project

A project is where Workforce resources live for a product, customer environment, or internal rollout. Create the project that will hold your first portal, integrations, skills, and access policies.

![Create your first project](/images/dashboard-audit/onboarding-create-project-filled.png)

## Open The Dashboard

After project creation, Metorial opens the project dashboard. From here you can open Workforce, create a portal, browse integrations, and review activity.

![Project dashboard home](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-home-after-onboarding.png)
