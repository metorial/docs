  - How to make an account
  - How to set up your workspace
  - How to choose a data region
  - How to create your first project
</Note>

## Creating your account

To start building with providers, you'll first need a Metorial account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Metorial Dashboard">
    If you already have an account, log in via the [Metorial Dashboard](https://platform.metorial.com).

    ![Metorial login](/images/dashboard-audit/auth-login.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting up an Organization and Project

After signing up, you'll be guided through setting up an organization and a project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Organization">
    An **organization** in Metorial is your workspace for projects, teams, and access policies. Choose the data region that fits your users and compliance needs.

    ![Create an organization and choose a data region](/images/dashboard-audit/onboarding-create-organization-eu.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Project">
    A **project** is a dedicated space within an organization where you can manage integration providers, deployments, and configurations.

    ![Create your first project](/images/dashboard-audit/onboarding-create-project-filled.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    After project creation, Metorial opens the project home page with a getting-started checklist and catalog shortcuts.

    ![Project dashboard home](/images/dashboard-audit/dashboard-home-after-onboarding.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
